Privacy Policy for Thrifty Piggy

This Privacy Policy explains how Thrifty Piggy handles user data, including the ability for users to email certain data from the app.


Data Collection and Usage

  • No Automatic Data Collection: Thrifty Piggy does not collect, store, or share any personal information automatically.

  • User-Initiated Data Sharing: The app allows users to email certain data directly from their device. This action is entirely user-initiated, and the app itself does not retain or transmit this data beyond the user's chosen email service.


Third-Party Services

When users email data, their information (e.g., email address and any included content) may be processed by their email service provider (e.g., Gmail, Yahoo). Users should consult their email provider's privacy policy for details on how their data is handled.


Links to Other Websites

Our Service may contain links to other websites that are not operated by Us. If You click on a third party link, You will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise You to review the Privacy Policy of every site You visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third party sites or services.


Data Storage and Security

The app does not store any user data on external servers. All data remains on the user's device unless explicitly shared via email.


User Rights

As no personal data is collected by Thrifty Piggy, users do not need to request access, correction, or deletion of stored data. However, users are responsible for managing any data they choose to send via email.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.


Contact Us
